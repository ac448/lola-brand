LOLA · Brand System · Component Spec 01

Status chips

Color carries state. Warm means LOLA routed it herself. Cool means a person reviewed it. Cream means a human needs to look. Anyone scanning a board can read the state of the queue without opening a single task.

The three states

Routed

state/routed · warm gradient

LOLA read the request and routed it to an owner. The pulsing dot marks a call a person has not reviewed yet.

Reviewed

state/reviewed · cool gradient

A person reviewed the call and kept it. Cool tones appear once a human has looked.

Needs human

state/human · navy on cream

The request is multi-format or unclear. LOLA moves it to Pending Info for a person instead of guessing. Flat color, no gradient.
